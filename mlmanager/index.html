ML MANAGER

A modern, easy and customizable app manager with Material Design for Android 4.0.3+. Available on Google Play.

Your device, your apps.

Manage your device apps. Mark them as favorite, organize your apps by installed and system apps.

Extract APKs.

Get the APK file of every of your installed & system apps or share it directly using Telegram, Dropbox, email, etc. Really simple.

Material Design.

ML Manager has been created following the Material Design guideline, allowing the user to customize the interface.

More with Pro and Root.

Enjoy all the features with Pro and Root, like hiding apps from the device launcher, cleaning app data or uninstalling system apps.

PRO Show only those applications you want.

If you have a rooted device then you have more. Do you want to hide some of your apps? This new feature of ML Manager hides the apps that you don't want to show on your device. Keep some of your apps in secret.

Beautiful. Simple. Easy.

Your apps can finally be well organized. Make searches by app name, sort your applications by name, size, installation and update date. Customize every color of ML Manager from the settings and get a fresh and unique interface.
